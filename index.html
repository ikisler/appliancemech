Welcome to Appliance Mechanics

Appliance Mechanics is a locally and family owned appliance repair business that has over 21 years of repair experience. Our factory trained and licensed technicians offer appliance repair and service for most major brands. Our technicians strive to have fully stocked vans; providing prompt and in most cases same day service so we can get your unit fixed quickly.

About Us

Most of our business is through word of mouth and repeat business from satisfied customers. That’s how we’ve built our wonderful reputation, one happy customer at a time. Our goal is to add you to our community of satisfied customers who make us their top choice for appliance repair.

Our Mission

Appliance Mechanic strives to bring our customers quick, professional and first time repairs. We vow to be honest and upfront with our prices – leaving no doubt in the total cost and the overall repair that you are receiving. Our intentions are to put the customer’s needs before our profit.
